# FINANCE TRACKER
This is a wealth management dashboard built entirely with pure React and vanilla CSS. This app doesn't only have normal finance tracking system but also an ai system which suggests you to do whats the best for you from the track records of the web app. It also helps the user track where the money is coming from, where it is going and gives an automated smart feedback on your habits. Also it tracks where the most money is coming from and where the most money is going from due to this its easy for the user to track and keep hold of what is happening and manage their finances.
# FEATURES
1.The core feature of this app remains the you can quickly log all your income streams (like your salary or side hustles) and your monthly expenses into clean and organinzed tables. You can delete row and add rows as per your need.

2.The code has a smart set of rules built directly into the architecture. As you add or change numbeers, the model constantly analyzes your spending habits in the background. It suggests you what to do with your money. As an example if your rent is too high compared to your earnings, it immediately pops up color-coded advice card saying what can you do to solve it.

3.The app also has the feature that calculates your total saving rates and shows it on a smooth visual progress ring. It also builds a custom bar chart of your expenses so you can see your biggest spending leaks at a glance, and it tells you how many months it will take to reach your ultimate saving goal.

# TECH STACK
The core stack and architecture of this app revolves around React.JSX. React 18+(Functional Components. Hooks, Context, useReducer) were used for the core logic and for styling Pure Vanilla CSS as an external CSS was used.

# FINALLY
The core principal and the reason for making this app is so people can anytime visit and find a way to manage their finances and get suggestions on improving their saving goals and track their incoming and outgoing flow. Also the app is made with love and self done code so the users can have good time managing their finances with decorative charts and good AI suggestions to improve their finances.
